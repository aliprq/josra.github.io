Birds of a Feather

The old English proverb “Birds of a feather flock together” refers to the fact that birds congregates with birds of the same species. In software development the term Birds of a Feather (BoF) is used to mean an informal discussion group.
   BoFs generally allow for more audience interaction than the panel discussions typically seen at conventions; the discussions are not completely unguided, though, as there is still a discussion leader.
